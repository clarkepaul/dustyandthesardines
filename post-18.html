A mini camping trip with a bunch of friends staying at the popular Mount Holdsworth campsite.

The majority of families staying in tents but a couple motorhomes. Pretty eciting times as this was Zoe's first trip ever in the bus at a sweet age of around 4 weeks.

This was Kim's first time sitting freely (without seatbelt) at the dinnette table because we've out grown the seating configuration of the bus, but no fret, there is another seat in the works. It's also legal for us to have unbelted passengers in the back because of the age of the bus (although we would prefer belts obvisouly).

I'm guessing there was at least 13 kids keeping us all busy, all up to something different and wanting different things.

Was a good time, Isaac got to use his new drop-from-ceiling bed for the first time, of which was a great place for kids to "hang" out.

Included: walks, swims, bonfire, games.
